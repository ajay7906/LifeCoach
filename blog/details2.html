5 Deep Truths About Self-Development I Discovered in My Journey as a Life Coach

1. Even if you are confident, you may still need and benefit from life coaching

There is “so much” that happens in life every day, and you can't always see your own blind spots. There is always a scope of making life better and uncomplicated.

2. "What you think, you become" is often overrated in being spoken about but underrated in being implemented

Very few people make an effort to understand the importance of inner work. If you make an effort to understand how coaching can expand your nervous system and impact the way you think and feel about challenges in life, you won't shy away from investing in your growth.

However, if you realize that you are unable to come out of the mess in life alone, still, you might not choose to invest in self-development because your mind will give you enough excuses such as:

  • Not having time :)
  • The pleasure of feeling like a victim in life :)
  • Looking selfish to prioritize self, because the education system never taught about internal growth, which is not tied to skills, degree, or formal education :)

3. Self-love and self-acceptance are overrated in terms of being spoken about but underrated in terms of being implemented in "actual life situations"

True self-development requires deeper understanding and actual application in life.

4. When you aren't willing to let go of your old habits, despite wanting to break free, something is still serving you

This requires deep mind work, which takes time but creates massive breakthroughs in the way you perceive the "confusion" surrounding the problem.

5. Inner work isn't only for people who are struggling with major emotional problems

It is for everyone to lead a more peaceful and joyful life! There is always a scope of growth.
